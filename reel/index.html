1. Introduction & Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how 觀影編年史 (Reel) ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. Reel is a personal film-viewing journal. This policy applies only to the Reel app and not to any third-party websites or services it may link to.

By using Reel, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

2. Data We Collect

Reel is designed to be privacy-first. The records you create — the films you log, your ratings, notes, and viewing dates — are stored only on your device (and, if you enable it, in your personal iCloud account). We do not have access to this content.

Categories under Apple's Privacy Nutrition Label:

  • User Content — your film log, ratings, and notes. Stored on-device / in your private iCloud; not transmitted to us.
  • Diagnostics — anonymous crash logs, only if you have enabled sharing of diagnostics in iOS settings. Not linked to your identity.

Reel does not require an account and does not collect your name, email, contacts, location, identifiers for advertising, or usage analytics.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Your film log and notes are used solely to provide the app's core function — keeping your personal viewing history on your device.
  • Diagnostics, if shared, are used only to fix crashes and improve stability.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking.

4. Third-Party SDKs & Services

  • Apple CloudKit (iCloud) — if you enable iCloud sync, your film log is stored in your own private iCloud database. This is governed by Apple's privacy policy and is not accessible to us. (Apple Privacy)
  • The Movie Database (TMDb) API — when you search for a film, your search query is sent to TMDb to return film information and artwork. We do not send any personal identifier with these requests. (TMDb Privacy Policy)

Reel does not integrate any advertising or analytics SDKs.

5. Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not collect your personal content, so we have nothing to share or sell. Film search queries are sent to TMDb only to retrieve film data, as described above.

We do not sell your personal information.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Because your data lives on your device (and your private iCloud), you are in control of it. To delete your records:

  • Delete individual entries within the app at any time, or
  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data, and remove Reel data from iCloud via Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage.

If you have any questions about deletion, contact privacy@lgt.wtf.

7. Your Rights

Because Reel keeps your content on your own device and iCloud, you can access, edit, export, or delete it directly at any time. To the extent applicable laws (such as the GDPR or CCPA/CPRA) grant additional rights, you may contact us at privacy@lgt.wtf. We do not sell your data.

8. Children's Privacy

Reel is a general-audience app and is not directed to children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. As the app stores content only on the user's own device, no such information is transmitted to us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, provide a more prominent notice within the app. Your continued use of Reel after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
