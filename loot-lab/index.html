1. Introduction & Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Loot Lab ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. It applies only to the Loot Lab app and not to any third-party websites or services that the app may link to.

By using Loot Lab, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

2. Data We Collect

Loot Lab collects the following categories of data, aligned with Apple's Privacy Nutrition Label classifications:

  • Identifiers — a device-generated identifier and, for advertising, the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) where you have granted permission. Used for tracking.
  • Usage Data — in-app actions, levels reached, and feature interactions. Linked to the device identifier; not linked to your real identity.
  • Diagnostics — crash logs and performance data to keep the app stable. Not linked to your identity.
  • Purchases — records of in-app purchases (processed by Apple; we do not receive your payment card details).

Loot Lab does not require an account and does not collect your name, email, contacts, precise location, or health data.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate core game features and save your progress.
  • To analyze gameplay and improve the app (analytics).
  • To display advertising, including personalized ads where you have opted in via App Tracking Transparency.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve stability.
  • To process in-app purchases.

If you do not grant tracking permission, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized using the IDFA.

4. Third-Party SDKs & Services

  • Google AdMob — advertising. May collect identifiers and usage data to serve and measure ads. (Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase Analytics — usage analytics. (Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash and diagnostics reporting. (Privacy Policy)
  • Apple In-App Purchase — payment processing handled entirely by Apple.

5. Third-Party Data Sharing

We share data only with the service providers listed above, solely to provide and improve the app (analytics, crash reporting, and advertising). These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies.

We do not sell your personal information.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Game progress is stored on your device and is removed when you delete the app. Analytics, diagnostics, and advertising data are retained by our service providers only as long as needed for the purposes described, in line with their retention policies.

To request deletion of any data associated with your device, contact us at privacy@lgt.wtf and we will respond within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your data, to data portability, and to object to or withdraw consent for certain processing (GDPR). California residents may request information about and deletion of their data and may opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information (CCPA/CPRA); note that we do not sell your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact privacy@lgt.wtf. You can also limit ad tracking via your device's privacy settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Loot Lab is not directed to children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact privacy@lgt.wtf.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, provide a more prominent notice within the app. Your continued use of Loot Lab after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
